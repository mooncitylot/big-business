Help

Goal: Maximize Reported Earnings and keep the SEC off your back.

  1. Cook the Books — click the green orb to mark imaginary profits to market. Each click also raises heat.
  2. Buy schemes in Creative Accounting. They earn passively, but most raise heat too.
  3. Launder the heat — Arthur Andersen, Offshore Shells & Lobbyists lower suspicion while you keep earning.
  4. Shred Documents — panic shred all the docs! Drops heat quick (short cooldown).
  5. Damage Control — one-time upgrades that permanently cut heat, buff shredding, soften SEC raids, or boost earnings.

Career Ladder — your title climbs in order, Underwriter to CEO, each time your peak earnings cross a new milestone. There's no skipping rungs.

Employee ID — print a badge with your name and a photo (camera or placeholder). Your job title updates on the badge as you climb the ladder. Edit it anytime from the ID Badge menu.

⚠ Heat hits 100% → SEC raid. They seize some earnings and reset heat to 50%. Earnings seized are worse every time.

GAME OVER happens two ways:
• 4 SEC investigations → federal raid, you're done.
• Stock gets DELISTED — share price falls to $0.26. Heat drags it down and each raid permanently dents it, so cook fast but keep the stock alive.

BIGB stock Bullish with peak earnings, bearish with heat and raids. Hit $0.26 and you're delisted (see above).
